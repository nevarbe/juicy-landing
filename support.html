Managing your subscription

To view, change, or cancel your subscription, open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions. You can also restore a previous purchase from inside JUICY under Settings, "Restore purchases".

Lost your progress?

JUICY stores your progress on your device. If you reinstalled the app or switched phones, your streak and collection may reset, but your subscription can be restored with "Restore purchases" in the app's Settings.
