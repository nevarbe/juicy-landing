Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026
The short version: JUICY does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything you do in the app stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no tracking.

This Privacy Policy explains how the JUICY - Glutemaxxing mobile application ("JUICY", "the app", "we", "us") handles your information. By using JUICY, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

None. JUICY does not ask you to create an account and does not collect any personally identifiable information. The name and workout preferences you enter during setup, along with your progress, streaks, coins and collection, are stored only on your own device and are never transmitted to us or anyone else.

Data Storage

All of your app data is saved locally on your device using your phone's standard storage. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it. We cannot access, recover, or restore it.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple and by our payment provider, RevenueCat, to confirm your subscription status. This process is handled entirely by Apple and RevenueCat under their own privacy policies. We never see or store your payment details, card numbers, or Apple ID.

  • Apple Privacy Policy: apple.com/legal/privacy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

Analytics and Tracking

JUICY contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software. We do not use cookies, device identifiers for tracking, or any behavioral profiling.

Children's Privacy

JUICY is not directed at children under 13. Because we collect no personal data, we do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
